<p><p>The medical sensing electrode market is categorized into two main types: disposable and reusable electrodes. Disposable electrodes are designed for single-use, offering convenience and reducing the risk of cross-contamination in clinical settings. They are commonly used in electrocardiography (ECG) and diagnostics. In contrast, reusable electrodes are durable, intended for multiple uses, and are often made from materials that can withstand sterilization processes. Both types serve critical roles in patient monitoring and diagnostics, each with unique applications and benefits.</p></p>
