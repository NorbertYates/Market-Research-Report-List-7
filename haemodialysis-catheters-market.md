The market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Cuffed Tunneling Catheters**: These are surgically placed, allowing long-term access while minimizing infection risk.</p><p>2. **Non-Cuffed Tunneling Catheters**: Similar to cuffed versions but without cuffs, used for temporary access with a slightly higher infection risk.</p><p>3. **Non-Tunneled Catheters**: Placed directly into a vein for short-term use, offering immediate access but higher infection rates, typically used in emergencies or short dialysis courses.</p></p>
