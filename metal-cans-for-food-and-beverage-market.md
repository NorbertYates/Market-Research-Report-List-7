<p><p>Metal cans for food and beverage packaging are primarily categorized into two types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are constructed from three separate components—two cylindrical bodies and a top lid—often used for canned foods like soups and vegetables. In contrast, two-piece cans are formed from a single metal sheet, creating a seamless cylinder with a bottom and lid, typically utilized for beverages like soft drinks and beer. Both designs offer durability, protection, and extended shelf life.</p></p>
