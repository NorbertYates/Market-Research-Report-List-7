<p><p>Dry running rotary vane vacuum pumps are categorized into single-stage and two-stage models based on their operational design. Single-stage pumps are simpler and suitable for applications requiring moderate vacuum levels, typically offering lower investment costs and ease of maintenance. In contrast, two-stage pumps provide deeper vacuum levels by utilizing two sets of vanes, making them ideal for demanding industrial processes. These distinctions cater to varying requirements in sectors like pharmaceuticals, food packaging, and electronics, ensuring optimal performance based on specific applications.</p></p>
