<p><p>Medical Practice Management Platforms are primarily categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions offer flexibility, allowing healthcare providers to access patient data anytime, anywhere, and often include automatic updates and scalability. They typically have lower upfront costs and reduce the need for extensive IT infrastructure. In contrast, on-premises platforms are installed locally within a facility, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types serve distinct operational needs.</p></p>
