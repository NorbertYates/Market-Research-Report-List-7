<p><ul><li>Conduction Type</li><li>Convection Type</li><li>Radiation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food dryer market is categorized into three main types: conduction, convection, and radiation. Conduction type dryers utilize direct contact between heating elements and food surfaces to transfer heat, leading to efficient drying but often slower processes. Convection type dryers circulate heated air around the food, promoting uniform drying at moderate speeds. Radiation type dryers use infrared waves to directly heat the food, providing rapid drying with minimal airflow. Each type addresses specific drying needs and efficiency preferences in food preservation.</p></p>
