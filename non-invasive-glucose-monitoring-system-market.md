<p><p>The Non-Invasive Glucose Monitoring System market comprises two primary types: wearable and non-wearable devices. Wearable devices, such as smartwatches and fitness trackers, continuously monitor glucose levels through skin contact, offering real-time data for users. Non-wearable devices, on the other hand, utilize techniques like spectrometry or imaging to assess glucose levels without skin contact, often through external devices or mobile applications. Both types aim to provide convenient and pain-free glucose monitoring solutions for diabetes management.</p></p>
