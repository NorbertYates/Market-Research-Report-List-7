<p><p>The walk-through metal detection market is primarily categorized into two types: LC Oscillating Metal Detectors and Pulse Induction (PI) Metal Detectors. LC Oscillating Metal Detectors utilize electromagnetic fields to detect metallic objects, providing quick and efficient scanning for security applications. In contrast, Pulse Induction Metal Detectors emit short, powerful pulses of energy, allowing for deeper penetration and the detection of buried or concealed metals. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing security at public venues, airports, and events.</p></p>
