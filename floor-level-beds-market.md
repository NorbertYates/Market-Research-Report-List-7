<p><p>The Floor Level Beds market comprises two main types: electrically operated and manually operated beds. Electrically operated floor level beds utilize motorized systems, allowing for easy adjustments in height and position at the touch of a button, enhancing patient comfort and caregiver convenience. Manually operated floor level beds, on the other hand, require physical adjustment through levers or cranks, offering a cost-effective solution but with less ease of use. Both types cater to different healthcare settings and patient needs.</p></p>
