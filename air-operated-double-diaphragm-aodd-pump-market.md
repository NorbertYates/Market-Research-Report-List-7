<p><p>An Air-Operated Double Diaphragm (AODD) Pump is a positive displacement pump that uses compressed air to move fluids. It features two diaphragms that alternate in movement to create a suction and discharge action, making it effective for handling various liquids, including slurries, viscous fluids, and chemicals. AODD pumps are valued for their versatility, ease of maintenance, and ability to run dry without damage, making them suitable for diverse industries such as chemical processing, food and beverage, and water treatment.</p><p>The AODD pump market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for efficient and reliable fluid transfer solutions across various sectors.
