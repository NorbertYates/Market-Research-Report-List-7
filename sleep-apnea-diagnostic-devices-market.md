<p><p>The sleep apnea diagnostic devices market can be categorized mainly into screening type devices and stereotype devices. Screening type devices include home sleep apnea testing systems that help identify potential sleep apnea cases through portable monitoring systems. These devices often provide initial assessments and are convenient for patient use in a home setting. On the other hand, stereotype devices involve more comprehensive evaluations, such as polysomnography conducted in sleep labs, offering detailed diagnostics for accurate sleep apnea assessment and treatment planning.</p></p>
