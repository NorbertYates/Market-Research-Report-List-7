<p><strong>The Hydraulic Rotary Unions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Passage</li><li>Single-Passage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic rotary unions market comprises two primary types: multi-passage and single-passage. Multi-passage rotary unions allow multiple fluid paths to simultaneously transfer hydraulic fluid, making them ideal for complex machinery requiring efficient fluid distribution. In contrast, single-passage rotary unions enable one fluid line connection, offering simplicity and reliability for applications with straightforward fluid transfer needs. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements of the application, such as space constraints and fluid flow complexity.</p></p>
