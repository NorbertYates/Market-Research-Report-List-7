<p><p>The standard spark plug market is divided into hot and cold spark plugs, catering to different engine types and operational conditions. Hot spark plugs operate at higher temperatures, promoting efficient combustion and reducing fouling in low-performance engines. They are ideal for everyday driving. In contrast, cold spark plugs are designed for high-performance or racing engines, dissipating heat quickly to prevent pre-ignition under extreme conditions. Engineers select the appropriate type based on the specific thermal requirements of the engine.</p></p>
