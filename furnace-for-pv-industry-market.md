<p><p>In the photovoltaic (PV) industry, the furnace market is primarily categorized into reduction furnaces and single crystal furnaces. Reduction furnaces are utilized for refining silicon, facilitating the removal of impurities to produce high-purity silicon essential for photovoltaic applications. Single crystal furnaces, on the other hand, are specifically designed for growing single crystal silicon ingots, enabling the production of high-efficiency solar cells. Both types of furnaces play crucial roles in enhancing the quality and performance of silicon-based solar technologies.</p></p>
