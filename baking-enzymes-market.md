<p><p>The baking enzymes market comprises three main types: carbohydrases, proteases, and lipases. Carbohydrases enhance dough fermentation and improve texture by breaking down carbohydrates, contributing to better flavor and volume. Proteases assist in the breakdown of gluten, resulting in improved dough extensibility and softness. Lipases are responsible for fat breakdown, enhancing flavor and shelf life of baked goods. Together, these enzymes play a crucial role in optimizing baking processes, ensuring consistent quality and product performance in the industry.</p></p>
