<p><p>The Synthetic Associative Thickener market is categorized into two primary types: Anionic and Nonionic thickeners. Anionic thickeners carry a negative charge and are commonly used in applications that require strong adhesion and stability, such as paints and coatings. Nonionic thickeners, on the other hand, do not carry any charge, making them versatile in various formulations, including personal care and cleaning products. Both types enhance viscosity and improve the performance of formulations across diverse industries.</p></p>
