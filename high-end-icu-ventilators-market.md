<p><ul><li>Invasive Ventilation</li><li>Non-invasive Ventilation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end ICU ventilators market is segmented into invasive and non-invasive ventilation types. Invasive ventilation involves the use of endotracheal tubes or tracheostomy to deliver air directly into the lungs, providing precise control for critically ill patients. Non-invasive ventilation, on the other hand, employs masks or similar devices to support patients without surgical procedures, making it less intrusive. Both types cater to varying patient needs, enhancing respiratory support in ICUs and improving outcomes for patients with severe respiratory conditions.</p></p>
