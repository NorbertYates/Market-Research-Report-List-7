<p><p>Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) technology is categorized into two primary market types: Standalone DPI and Integrated DPI. Standalone DPI solutions function independently, primarily focusing on analyzing network traffic for security, performance monitoring, and regulatory compliance. In contrast, Integrated DPI is embedded within larger network systems and equipment, such as routers or switches, providing real-time traffic analysis alongside other functionalities. Both types enhance network management but differ in deployment and operational scope, with standalone offering dedicated functionality and integrated providing a holistic approach.</p></p>
