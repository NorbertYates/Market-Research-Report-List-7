<p><p>The Portable Ultra Violet (UV) Light market is divided into three main types: UVA, UVB, and UVC lights. UVA lights emit long wavelengths and are commonly used for tanning and curing applications. UVB lights, with medium wavelengths, are primarily used in therapy and certain industrial processes. UVC lights, emitting the shortest wavelengths, are highly effective for disinfection and sterilization purposes, particularly in healthcare and water treatment settings. Each type serves distinct applications based on their unique properties and effects.</p></p>
