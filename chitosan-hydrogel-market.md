<p><p>The Chitosan Hydrogel market is categorized into two types: injection type and non-injection type. Injection type hydrogels are designed for direct delivery into tissues or wounds, providing localized treatment and potentially enhancing healing processes. Non-injection type hydrogels, on the other hand, are used in applications such as wound dressings and drug delivery systems that can be applied externally. Both types leverage the biocompatibility and biodegradability of chitosan, making them popular in medical and pharmaceutical fields.</p></p>
