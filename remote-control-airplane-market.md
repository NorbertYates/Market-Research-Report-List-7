<p><p>The remote control airplane market is categorized by materials used for construction. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) is lightweight, cost-effective, and offers good insulation, making it popular for beginners. EPP (Expanded Polypropylene) is more durable and flexible, allowing for better crash resistance, thus favored by experienced pilots. EPO (Expanded Polyolefin) combines lightweight characteristics with strength, appealing to high-performance models. The "Others" category includes alternative materials like carbon fiber or fiberglass, catering to niche markets and specialized aircraft designs.</p></p>
