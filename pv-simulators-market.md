<p><p>PV simulators are classified into several market types based on the light source used. Metal halide lamps provide a broad spectrum of light, simulating solar conditions effectively. Carbon arc lamps produce intense and stable illumination, making them suitable for high-accuracy simulations. Tungsten lamps, known for their warm light, are used for specific testing applications due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness.
