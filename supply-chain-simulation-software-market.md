<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based Supply Chain Simulation Software</li><li>On-Premise Supply Chain Simulation Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Supply Chain Simulation Software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based supply chain simulation software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access it via the internet, promoting flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premise supply chain simulation software is installed locally on a company’s hardware, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types aim to optimize supply chain operations through advanced modeling and analysis.</p></p>
