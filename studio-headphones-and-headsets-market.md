<p><p>The studio headphones and headsets market is categorized into three primary types: closed-back, semi-open back, and fully-open back designs. Closed-back headphones offer isolation from external sounds, making them ideal for tracking and mixing in noisy environments. Semi-open back models provide a balance, allowing some ambient sound while maintaining sound quality. Fully-open back headphones deliver an expansive soundstage and natural audio but leak sound and allow external noise, making them suitable for critical listening in quiet settings.</p></p>
