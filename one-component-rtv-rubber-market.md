<p><ul><li>Addition Type</li><li>Condensation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The one-component RTV (Room Temperature Vulcanizing) rubber market is divided into two main types: addition type and condensation type. Addition type RTV rubber cures through a hydrosilylation reaction, resulting in low volatility and excellent mechanical properties, making it ideal for intricate applications. In contrast, condensation type RTV rubber cures through a reaction that releases byproducts, such as alcohol or water, which can affect curing time and strength. Each type serves distinct applications, depending on the required performance characteristics.</p></p>
