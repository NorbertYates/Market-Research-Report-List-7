<p><p>The active wound care market comprises various types, primarily focusing on dressings and grafts. Dressings are advanced materials designed to promote healing by maintaining a moist environment, managing exudate, and providing protection against infection. They include hydrocolloids, alginates, and foam dressings. Grafts, on the other hand, involve the transplantation of skin or biological tissues to facilitate wound closure and repair. Both categories are essential in managing chronic and acute wounds, enhancing patient outcomes through improved healing processes.</p></p>
