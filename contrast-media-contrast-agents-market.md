<p><p>Contrast media, also known as contrast agents, enhance the clarity of medical imaging by improving the differentiation of tissues and blood vessels. The market is categorized into three main types: X-ray and CT contrast media, which primarily use iodinated compounds to highlight structures; MRI contrast media, often based on gadolinium, that improves soft tissue visualization; and ultrasound contrast media, which utilize microbubbles to enhance ultrasound images. Each type serves specific diagnostic purposes, contributing to improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
