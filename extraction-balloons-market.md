Triple-lumen balloons have three channels, allowing simultaneous inflation, deflation, and aspiration, optimizing fluid removal and enhancing maneuverability. Both types cater to different clinical needs, ensuring efficient extraction procedures in various medical contexts, including cardiology and vascular surgery.</p></p>
