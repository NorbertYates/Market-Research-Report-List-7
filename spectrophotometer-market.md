<p><ul><li>UV-Visible Spectrophotometer</li><li>IR Spectrophotometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spectrophotometer market is primarily categorized into UV-Visible and IR spectrophotometers. UV-Visible spectrophotometers are used for analyzing absorbance and transmittance in the ultraviolet to visible light range, making them essential in chemical analysis, biology, and environmental monitoring. In contrast, IR spectrophotometers operate in the infrared range, enabling the identification of molecular structures and functional groups in various materials. Both types play crucial roles in research, quality control, and industrial applications, driving advancements in analytical techniques.</p></p>
