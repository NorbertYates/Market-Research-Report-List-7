<p><p>The fermented bean curd market primarily consists of two main types: white preserved bean curd and red fermented bean curd. White preserved bean curd is typically milder in flavor and is made from soybeans that have been fermented and then cured in a brine solution. In contrast, red fermented bean curd is characterized by its vibrant color and stronger taste, often enhanced with spices or rice wine. Both types are popular in Asian cuisines for their unique flavors and culinary versatility.</p></p>
