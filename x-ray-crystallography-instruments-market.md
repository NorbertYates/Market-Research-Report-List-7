<p><p>The X-Ray Crystallography Instruments market comprises two main types: X-Ray Powder Diffraction (XRPD) and Single-Crystal X-Ray Diffraction (SCXRD). XRPD analyzes powdered samples to determine the crystalline structure and phase identification by measuring the diffraction patterns of X-rays. In contrast, SCXRD examines single crystal samples, providing detailed information about the geometry, atomic arrangement, and bonding in a crystal. Both techniques are vital in material science, chemistry, and biology for characterizing complex structures at the atomic level.</p></p>
