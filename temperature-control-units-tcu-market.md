<p><p>Temperature Control Units (TCUs) are essential devices used to regulate the temperature of industrial processes, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency. The market comprises primarily two types: Water Temperature Control Units and Oil Temperature Control Units. Water TCUs utilize water as a coolant, making them ideal for applications requiring precise temperature management in various industries. In contrast, Oil TCUs use oil-based fluids, providing higher temperature resistance and better thermal stability, suitable for high-performance applications where greater heat transfer is essential.</p></p>
