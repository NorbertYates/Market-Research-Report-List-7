<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Inspection/Review Equipment market focuses on two primary methodologies: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method compares masks directly against each other to detect defects, ensuring consistency across production runs. Meanwhile, the DB method involves comparing current die images against a stored database of known good images, facilitating identification of discrepancies. Both methods are essential for enhancing manufacturing quality and reliability in semiconductor production, resulting in improved yield and performance.</p></p>
