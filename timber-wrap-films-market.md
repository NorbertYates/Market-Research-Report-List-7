<p><ul><li>HDPE Wrap Films</li><li>LDPE Wrap Films</li><li>PP Wrap Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Timber Wrap Films market comprises three primary types: HDPE, LDPE, and PP wrap films. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) wrap films are known for their strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) wrap films offer flexibility and resistance to moisture, ideal for protecting timber products during storage and transport. PP (Polypropylene) wrap films provide excellent puncture resistance and clarity, commonly used for packaging and bundling timber efficiently. Each type caters to specific protection and performance needs in the timber industry.</p></p>
