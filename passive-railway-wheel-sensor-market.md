<p><p>The Passive Railway Wheel Sensor market includes two main types: Single Wheel Sensors and Double Wheel Sensors. Single Wheel Sensors are designed to monitor the condition and performance of individual wheels, detecting issues such as wear or imbalance. In contrast, Double Wheel Sensors are employed to assess pairs of wheels, offering a broader perspective on wheelset dynamics and enhancing overall safety. Both types utilize passive technology, providing reliable data without the need for external power sources, making them cost-effective solutions for railway operators.</p></p>
