<p><ul><li>Natural Pozzolana</li><li>Artificial Pozzolana</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portland Pozzolan Cement (PPC) is categorized into two main types: natural pozzolana and artificial pozzolana. Natural pozzolana is derived from volcanic ash or other natural sources, enhancing the cement’s strength and durability when mixed with ordinary Portland cement. In contrast, artificial pozzolana is manufactured materials, such as fly ash or silica fume, designed to mimic the properties of natural pozzolana. Both types improve the performance of concrete, offering benefits such as reduced permeability and increased resistance to chemical attacks.</p></p>
