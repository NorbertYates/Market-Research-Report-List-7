<p><p>In the packaging market, hot runner systems are crucial for improving efficiency and quality in injection molding processes. Open gate systems allow direct material flow into the molds, promoting faster cycle times but can result in visible gate marks. In contrast, valve gate systems precisely control the flow of molten material, ensuring clean finishes and reducing waste. They enable better control over gate sealing and can improve aesthetics in packaging applications, making both types valuable depending on specific product requirements.</p></p>
