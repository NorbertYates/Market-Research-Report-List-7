<p><strong>The Nuclear Molecular Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positron Emission Tomography</li><li>Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography</li><li>Planar Scintigraphy Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nuclear Molecular Imaging System market comprises three main types: Positron Emission Tomography (PET), which provides detailed 3D images by detecting gamma rays emitted from positron annihilation; Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT), which uses gamma cameras to create 3D images by tracking photons emitted from radiotracers; and Planar Scintigraphy Systems, which offer two-dimensional images of gamma radiation from radiotracers. Each modality plays a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various diseases, especially in oncology and cardiology.</p></p>
