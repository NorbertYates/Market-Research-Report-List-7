Electric tables offer precise adjustments for height and position using an electric motor, while hydraulic tables utilize fluid pressure for similar adjustments. Manual tables require manual adjustments and are generally simpler, catering to specific surgical needs. Each type serves unique operational requirements in urological surgeries.</p></p>
