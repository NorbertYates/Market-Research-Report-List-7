<p><p>The electric pressure washer market is categorized into three main types: light duty, medium duty, and heavy duty. Light duty models, typically under 2000 PSI, are ideal for small tasks like cleaning cars and patios. Medium duty models, ranging from 2000 to 2800 PSI, are suited for more robust cleaning tasks like driveways and outdoor furniture. Heavy duty pressure washers, exceeding 2800 PSI, are designed for industrial use, tackling tough surfaces such as concrete and heavy machinery. Each type caters to specific cleaning needs based on power and application.</p></p>
