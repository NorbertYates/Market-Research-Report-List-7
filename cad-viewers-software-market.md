<p><p>CAD viewers software is available in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise CAD viewers are installed directly on local machines, offering users complete control over data security and performance. However, they may require significant hardware resources and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based CAD viewers operate over the internet, enabling access from any device with a web browser. This model offers scalability and ease of collaboration among teams, although it relies on a stable internet connection and raises concerns about data security.</p></p>
