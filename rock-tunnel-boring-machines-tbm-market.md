<p><p>Rock Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are specialized equipment used for excavation in hard rock environments. The market features two main types: Open-type TBMs and Shielded Hard Rock TBMs. Open-type TBMs are suited for soft soil conditions, allowing for efficient tunnel excavation, while Shielded Hard Rock TBMs are designed to handle high-pressure rock formations, offering enhanced protection and stability for workers. Both types cater to diverse geological conditions and project requirements, playing a crucial role in underground construction.</p></p>
