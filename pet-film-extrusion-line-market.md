<p><p>The PET film extrusion line market is segmented into multilayer and single-layer types. Single-layer PET films are produced using a singular layer of polyethylene terephthalate, offering cost-effective solutions for various applications. In contrast, multilayer PET films consist of multiple layers, enhancing barrier properties, durability, and overall performance, making them suitable for specialized applications like packaging and electronics. Both types serve distinct market needs, with single-layer films appealing to budget-conscious buyers, while multilayer films cater to higher-end, performance-driven requirements.</p></p>
