<p><ul><li>Hot Die Casting</li><li>Cold Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft and Aerospace Die Casting Components market includes two main types: hot die casting and cold die casting. Hot die casting involves pouring molten metal into preheated molds, allowing for intricate designs and high-quality finishes, ideal for aerospace applications requiring heat resistance. Cold die casting, on the other hand, uses lower temperatures and pressures, resulting in faster production cycles and cost efficiency, making it suitable for less complex components. Both methods cater to the industry's demand for precision and durability.</p></p>
