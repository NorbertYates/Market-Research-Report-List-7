<p><p>Hopper cars are specialized railcars designed for transporting bulk materials. There are two main types: covered and open hopper cars. Covered hopper cars are equipped with roofs to protect cargo from weather, making them ideal for commodities like grain, plastic pellets, and cement. Open hopper cars, on the other hand, lack a cover, allowing for easy loading and unloading of bulk materials like coal and gravel. Both types play vital roles in freight transportation, catering to different cargo needs.</p></p>
