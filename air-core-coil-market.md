<p><p>The air core coil market is categorized primarily into two types: circular and spiral coils. Spiral air core coils, characterized by their helical design, provide a compact form factor, making them suitable for miniaturized electronic devices. Both types serve critical roles in inductors, transformers, and other electromagnetic applications, highlighting their importance in various technological sectors.</p></p>
