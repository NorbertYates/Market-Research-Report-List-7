<p><p>The Blockchain-based Service Network (BSN) comprises two primary market types: permissionless and permissioned blockchains. Permissionless blockchains are open platforms that allow anyone to participate, validate transactions, and access services, fostering transparency and decentralization. In contrast, permissioned blockchains restrict access to selected participants, offering enhanced control, privacy, and compliance for businesses and organizations. Each type serves distinct use cases, with permissionless blockchains favoring innovation and decentralization, while permissioned blockchains prioritize security and regulatory adherence in enterprise environments.</p></p>
