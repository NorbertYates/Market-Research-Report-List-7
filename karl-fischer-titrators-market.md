<p><strong>The Karl Fischer Titrators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulometric Titration</li><li>Volumetric Titration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Karl Fischer titrator market is divided into two main types: coulometric titration and volumetric titration. Coulometric titration is used for low moisture content samples, where the amount of water is determined by measuring the current produced during the electrochemical reaction. Volumetric titration, on the other hand, involves adding a reagent to the sample until the endpoint is reached, making it suitable for samples with higher moisture levels. Both methods are essential for precise moisture analysis in various industries.</p></p>
