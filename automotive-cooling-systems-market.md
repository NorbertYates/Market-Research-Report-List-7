<p><p>The automotive cooling systems market is mainly divided into two types: liquid-cooled and air-cooled systems. Liquid-cooled systems utilize coolant to absorb and dissipate heat from the engine, providing efficient temperature regulation, especially in high-performance vehicles. In contrast, air-cooled systems rely on air to maintain engine temperatures, making them simpler and lighter, but potentially less effective in extreme conditions. Each system has its advantages, influencing vehicle design and performance, catering to different needs within the automotive industry.</p></p>
