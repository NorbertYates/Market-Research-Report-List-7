<p><p>The hygiene adhesives market can be categorized into woven and non-woven types based on their fabric structure. Woven hygiene adhesives utilize interlaced fibers, providing strength and durability, making them suitable for applications like adult incontinence products and feminine hygiene. Non-woven hygiene adhesives, on the other hand, consist of bonded fibers, offering softness and flexibility, ideal for products such as baby diapers and feminine care items. Both types play vital roles in ensuring comfort and performance in personal care products.</p></p>
