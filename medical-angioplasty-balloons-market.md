<p><p>The medical angioplasty balloons market consists of several types catering to different therapeutic needs. Normal balloons are used for basic balloon angioplasty to widen narrowed arteries. Drug-eluting balloons incorporate medications that help prevent re-narrowing by releasing drugs during inflation. Cutting balloons have microscopic blades to facilitate the dilation of tougher lesions. Scoring balloons feature indentations that create micro-incisions in the plaque, enhancing vessel expansion. Each type offers unique advantages, addressing varying degrees of arterial blockage and patient profiles.</p></p>
