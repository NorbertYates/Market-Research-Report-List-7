<p><p>A sheath catheter is a specialized medical device designed for various vascular access procedures, primarily used in interventional cardiology and radiology. It facilitates the insertion of other catheters or instruments into blood vessels, providing a means of safe and effective access for diagnostic or therapeutic interventions. The sheath acts as a protective lining, preventing damage to the vessel while enabling easy navigation and maneuverability within the vascular system.</p><p>The sheath catheter market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in medical technology, and a growing aging population.
