<p><ul><li>Fusion Method</li><li>Bonding Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminated steel panel market is characterized by two primary types: the fusion method and bonding agent applications. The fusion method involves welding steel layers under high heat to create a solid, durable panel. In contrast, bonding agents utilize adhesives to securely join steel layers, offering flexibility in design and manufacturing. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs, such as construction and automotive industries, providing options for strength, weight, and cost, while enhancing aesthetic appeal and performance.</p></p>
