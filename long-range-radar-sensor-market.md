<p><p>The long-range radar sensor market primarily consists of two types: Long Range Millimeter Wave Radar Sensors and Long Range Laser Radar Sensors. Long Range Millimeter Wave Radar Sensors utilize millimeter wave frequencies for accurate detection and tracking over significant distances, making them ideal for applications like automotive safety and drone monitoring. In contrast, Long Range Laser Radar Sensors, or LiDAR, employ laser beams to create precise 3D maps and detect obstacles, extensively used in autonomous vehicles and surveying technologies.</p></p>
