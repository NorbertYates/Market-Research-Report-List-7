<p><p>The MEMS inclinometer market is primarily categorized into single-axis and dual-axis devices. Single-axis MEMS inclinometers measure tilt in one direction, making them suitable for applications like structural health monitoring and automotive systems. In contrast, dual-axis MEMS inclinometers detect tilt in both horizontal and vertical planes, providing comprehensive orientation data. These devices are widely used in industries such as construction, aerospace, and robotics, where precise angle measurement is crucial for performance and safety.</p></p>
