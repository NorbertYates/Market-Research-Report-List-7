<p><p>The silicone additives market includes various types, each serving distinct functions. Defoamers reduce foam during manufacturing processes, enhancing product stability. Wetting and dispersing agents improve the distribution of particles in formulations, ensuring uniform consistency. Rheology modifiers adjust the viscosity and flow characteristics of materials, optimizing application performance. Surfactants decrease surface tension, promoting better interaction between components, while lubricating agents enhance the ease of processing and improve the end-product's properties. Collectively, these additives enhance performance across diverse industries.</p></p>
