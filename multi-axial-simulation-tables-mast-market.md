<p><p>Multi-Axial Simulation Tables (MAST) are advanced systems for testing materials and structures under complex loading conditions. The market is segmented into electric and hydraulic simulation tables. Electric simulation tables utilize electric motors for precise control and are favored for their energy efficiency and lower maintenance. In contrast, hydraulic simulation tables rely on fluid power, offering higher force output and robustness for larger specimens. Both types cater to industries requiring detailed analysis of dynamic responses and structural integrity under various stress conditions.</p></p>
