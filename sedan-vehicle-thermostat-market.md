<p><p>The sedan vehicle thermostat market is segmented into insert thermostats and housing thermostats. In contrast, housing thermostats are integrated within a housing assembly, providing a compact design that simplifies installation. Both types play critical roles in maintaining optimal engine temperatures, reducing emissions, and improving fuel efficiency, driving their demand as automotive technology evolves.</p></p>
