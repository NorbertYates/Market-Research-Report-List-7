<p><ul><li>Convex Lenses</li><li>Convex Concave Lenses</li><li>Concave Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass aspheric lenses market comprises various types, including convex lenses, concave lenses, and convex-concave lenses. Convex lenses are thicker at the center, converging light rays to create a focused image, commonly used in cameras and eyewear. Concave lenses are thinner in the center, diverging light rays, ideal for correcting nearsightedness. Convex-concave lenses combine both shapes to manage optical aberrations, offering versatile applications in microscopy, photography, and advanced imaging systems, enhancing performance and image quality across diverse industries.</p></p>
