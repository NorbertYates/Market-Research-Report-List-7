<p><strong>The Micro Water Spraying Fertilizer Integrated Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Irrigation</li><li>Under Film Irrigation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro Water Spraying Fertilizer Integrated Machine market comprises two main types: Film Irrigation and Under Film Irrigation. Film Irrigation uses a plastic film layer placed on the soil surface to optimize water retention and nutrient delivery, enhancing plant growth while minimizing evaporation. In contrast, Under Film Irrigation involves placing the irrigation system beneath the film, allowing for efficient moisture and fertilizer distribution directly to the root zone. Both methods maximize resource use, improve crop yield, and promote sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
