<p><ul><li>Fillable Autoinjectors</li><li>Prefilled Autoinjectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The autoinjectors market comprises two primary types: fillable autoinjectors and prefilled autoinjectors. Fillable autoinjectors allow patients or healthcare providers to manually fill the injector with the required medication before use, offering flexibility in medication choice. In contrast, prefilled autoinjectors come preloaded with a specific medication, facilitating ease of use and reducing preparation time. Both types enhance patient compliance by providing a convenient and safe method for self-administration of injectable therapies, particularly for chronic conditions.</p></p>
