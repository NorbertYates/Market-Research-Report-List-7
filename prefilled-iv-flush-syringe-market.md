<p><ul><li>Saline IV Flush Syringe</li><li>Heparin IV Flush Syringe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prefilled IV flush syringe market includes two primary types: saline IV flush syringes and heparin IV flush syringes. Saline IV flush syringes are filled with sterile saline solution, commonly used to maintain venous patency and prevent clot formation in IV lines. In contrast, heparin IV flush syringes contain heparin, an anticoagulant that helps prevent thrombosis during and after IV therapy. Both types improve efficiency, reduce contamination risks, and enhance patient safety in clinical settings.</p></p>
