<p><p>The nutraceutical excipients market is categorized into two main types: dry and liquid excipients. Dry excipients include powders and granules used as fillers, binders, or disintegrants in tablet formulations, enhancing stability and dosage precision. Liquid excipients, on the other hand, are used in the formulation of suspensions, syrups, and emulsions, providing viscosity, solubility, and stabilization. Both types play crucial roles in the delivery and effectiveness of nutraceuticals, ensuring product quality and consumer safety.</p></p>
