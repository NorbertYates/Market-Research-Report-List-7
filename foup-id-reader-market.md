<p><p>The FOUP ID Reader market is categorized into two main types: Low-Frequency (LF) RFID Readers and High-Frequency (HF) RFID Readers. Low-Frequency RFID Readers typically operate at 125 kHz and are ideal for short-range applications with limited data transfer needs, making them suitable for tracking assets in controlled environments. In contrast, High-Frequency RFID Readers operate at 13.56 MHz, allowing for increased data capacity and faster read rates, making them better suited for applications requiring real-time inventory management and enhanced security features.</p></p>
