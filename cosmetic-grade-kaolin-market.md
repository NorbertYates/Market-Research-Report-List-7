<p><p>The cosmetic grade kaolin market encompasses three main types: hard kaolin, soft kaolin, and sandy kaolin. Hard kaolin, also known as "china clay," is characterized by its fine texture and high purity, ideal for products like face masks and powders. Soft kaolin has a more absorbent nature, often used in skincare for its gentle properties. Sandy kaolin contains larger particle sizes and impurities, typically utilized in formulations needing texture or exfoliation. Each type serves specific cosmetic applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
