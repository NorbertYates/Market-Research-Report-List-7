<p><p>The electric piston pump market is categorized primarily into two types: the 2-ball piston pump and the 4-ball piston pump. The 2-ball piston pump utilizes two balls to create fluid flow, resulting in a compact design suitable for lower pressure applications. In contrast, the 4-ball piston pump employs four balls, allowing for higher pressure capabilities and improved efficiency, making it ideal for demanding industrial applications. Both types are widely used in various sectors, including oil and gas, manufacturing, and water treatment.</p></p>
