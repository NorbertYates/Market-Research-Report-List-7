<p><p>Industrial wireless in discrete industries encompasses various market types designed for specific applications. Wireless Local Area Networks (WLAN) facilitate high-speed data transfer in confined areas, ideal for machinery communication. Wireless Personal Area Networks (WPAN) connect devices over short distances, suitable for sensor networks. Satellite systems (GNSS) provide positioning and tracking capabilities across vast areas. Low-Power Wide-Area Networks (LPWAN) allow long-range communications with minimal power for IoT devices. Cellular networks offer robust, wide coverage suitable for mobile operations and real-time data exchange.</p></p>
