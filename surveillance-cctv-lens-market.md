<p><p>The surveillance (CCTV) lens market comprises three main types: fixed lenses, varifocal lenses, and zoom lenses. Fixed lenses have a predetermined focal length, offering a stable field of view ideal for specific monitoring needs. Varifocal lenses allow users to adjust the focal length effortlessly, providing flexibility for capturing different perspectives. Zoom lenses offer a greater range of focal lengths, enabling significant image magnification without compromising quality, making them suitable for dynamic environments where detail retrieval is crucial.</p></p>
