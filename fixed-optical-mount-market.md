They facilitate easy adjustments and secure positioning in research and industrial applications. Post Mountable types, on the other hand, attach directly to optical posts, offering a robust solution for mounting optics in alignment systems. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring precise positioning and alignment in optical experiments.</p></p>
