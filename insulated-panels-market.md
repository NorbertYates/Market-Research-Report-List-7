<p><p>The insulated panels market comprises various types, each serving specific insulation needs. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) panels are lightweight, cost-effective, and provide good thermal insulation, making them popular for residential and commercial use. PUR/PIR (Polyurethane/Polyisocyanurate) panels offer superior thermal performance and are often used in industrial applications. Mineral and glass wool panels are known for their fire resistance and sound insulation properties, commonly utilized in commercial buildings and infrastructure projects where fire safety is a concern.</p></p>
