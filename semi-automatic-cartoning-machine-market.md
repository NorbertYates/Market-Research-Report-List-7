<p><strong>The Semi-Automatic Cartoning Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic cartoning machine market is divided into two main types: Vertical Form-Fill-Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form-Fill-Seal (H-FFS) machines. V-FFS machines create packages from a flat roll of film and fill them vertically, suitable for products with varying shapes and sizes, often used in food and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, H-FFS machines operate horizontally, efficiently filling and sealing packages, typically for stable items like cartons or boxes, enhancing production speed and reducing manual effort in packaging processes.</p></p>
