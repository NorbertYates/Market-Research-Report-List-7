<p><p>The Radio Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) Scanner market is segmented into two main types: Dual Collimator and Single Collimator. Dual Collimator scanners utilize two collimators to enhance sensitivity and resolution, allowing for improved detection of radioactively labeled compounds. In contrast, Single Collimator scanners use a single collimator, offering a more compact design and lower cost, but with limited sensitivity compared to their dual counterparts. Both types serve critical roles in analytical chemistry for radiolabeled sample analysis.</p></p>
