<p><p>The Food Grade Bentonite market is primarily divided into Sodium Bentonite and Calcium Bentonite types. Sodium Bentonite, known for its high absorbency and swelling properties, is commonly used in food processing as a clarifying agent and for decolorization. In contrast, Calcium Bentonite, with its lower swelling capacity, is valued for its binding and thickening properties, making it suitable for applications in food additives and dietary supplements. Both types are essential for ensuring food quality and safety.</p></p>
