<p><p>The Shoulder Elbow Wrist Hand Orthosis (SEWHO) market comprises two main types of devices: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to facilitate movement and promote functional use of the affected limbs, often incorporating springs or elastic components to support rehabilitation. In contrast, static orthotics provide rigid support and immobilization to stabilize joints, reduce pain, and prevent further injury. Both types aim to enhance the recovery process for patients with upper extremity conditions.</p></p>
