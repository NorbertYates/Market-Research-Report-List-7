<p><strong>The EMG Biosensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Biosensors</li><li>Intramuscular Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMG biosensor market is segmented into surface biosensors and intramuscular biosensors. Surface biosensors are non-invasive devices placed on the skin to detect electrical signals from muscles, commonly used in rehabilitation and sports medicine. They provide ease of use and comfort, making them suitable for long-term monitoring. In contrast, intramuscular biosensors are implanted within the muscle tissue, offering precise measurements and enhanced signal clarity. They are often utilized in advanced clinical applications for detailed muscle function assessment.</p></p>
