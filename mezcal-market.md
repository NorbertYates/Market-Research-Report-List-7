<p><strong>The Mezcal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Joven</li><li>Reposado</li><li>Anejo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mezcal market is primarily categorized into three types: Joven, Reposado, and Añejo. Joven mezcal is unaged, showcasing the raw agave flavor and often bottled right after distillation. Reposado is aged for at least two months, allowing a blend of agave and wood flavors to develop, while Añejo is aged for a minimum of one year, resulting in a smoother, richer profile with deeper oak influences. Each type offers a unique tasting experience catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
