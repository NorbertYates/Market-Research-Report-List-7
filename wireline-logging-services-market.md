<p><strong>The Wireline Logging Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cased Hole Wireline Logging Services</li><li>Open Hole Wireline Logging Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireline logging services market is divided into two primary types: cased hole and open hole logging services. Cased hole wireline logging involves operations conducted in previously drilled and completed wells, focusing on assessing the integrity and performance of casing and stimulation of hydrocarbon production. In contrast, open hole wireline logging pertains to evaluating uncased boreholes, providing insights into formation characteristics, porosity, and fluid saturation. Both types are essential for optimizing oil and gas extraction, enhancing reservoir management, and informing drilling decisions.</p></p>
