<p><p>The drilling fluid additives market encompasses a range of products that enhance the performance and efficiency of drilling operations. Fluid viscosifiers increase the thickness of drilling fluids, improving wellbore stability. Corrosion inhibitors protect equipment from degradation. Dispersants help maintain particle suspension, while surface modifiers improve fluid behavior at solid interfaces. Defoamers reduce foam formation, ensuring smooth fluid flow, and biocides prevent microbial contamination, safeguarding the integrity of the fluid. Together, these additives optimize drilling performance and extend equipment life.</p></p>
