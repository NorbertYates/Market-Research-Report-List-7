<p><ul><li>Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li><li>Not Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Waterborne Polyester Resins market is primarily divided into two types: Saturated and Unsaturated Waterborne Polyester Resins. Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins are characterized by their stable molecular structure, leading to improved chemical resistance and durability, making them suitable for a variety of applications such as coatings and adhesives. Unsaturated Waterborne Polyester Resins, on the other hand, contain reactive double bonds that facilitate cross-linking during curing processes, enhancing mechanical properties and enabling their use in construction and automotive industries.</p></p>
