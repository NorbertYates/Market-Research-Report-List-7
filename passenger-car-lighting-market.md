<p><p>The passenger car lighting market features three primary types: Xenon, Halogen, and LED lights. Xenon lights, known for their bright output and long lifespan, utilize gas discharge technology, making them a popular choice for high-performance vehicles. Halogen lights are commonly used due to their affordability and ease of replacement, providing reliable illumination. LED lights, favored for their energy efficiency and durability, offer versatile designs and long-lasting performance, increasingly becoming the standard in modern vehicles for both headlights and interior lighting.</p></p>
