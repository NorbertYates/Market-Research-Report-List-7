<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle keyless entry systems market is primarily divided into two types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start vehicles automatically when they approach, utilizing advanced sensors and proximity technology. In contrast, RKES requires drivers to use a remote control to lock/unlock doors and start the engine from a distance. Both systems enhance convenience and security, catering to the evolving demands of the commercial vehicle sector.</p></p>
