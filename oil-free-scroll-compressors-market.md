<p><p>The Oil-Free Scroll Compressors market is primarily divided into two types: Simplex and Multiplex. Simplex Type compressors consist of a single pair of scrolls, suitable for applications requiring a consistent flow of compressed air or gas with lower capacity demands. In contrast, Multiplex Type compressors feature multiple scroll sets working in tandem, allowing for higher capacities and enhanced efficiency. Both types provide oil-free compression, making them ideal for sensitive applications in industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and electronics.</p></p>
