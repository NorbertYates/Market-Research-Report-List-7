<p><p>Common mode electronic filters are used to suppress noise and interference in electronic signals. The market includes high-pass filters, which allow frequencies above a certain cutoff to pass while blocking lower frequencies; low-pass filters, which do the opposite by allowing lower frequencies to pass while blocking higher ones; and band-pass filters, which permit a specific range of frequencies to pass, filtering out both lower and higher frequencies. These filters are essential for enhancing signal integrity in various electronic applications.</p></p>
